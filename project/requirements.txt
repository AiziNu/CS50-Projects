# Deposit Calculator

This is a simple deposit calculator that allows users to deposit money into an account and check their balance.


## Features

- Deposit money into an account.
- Check the current balance.
- Simple and user-friendly interface.

## Usage

When you run the program, you will be prompted to enter the account owner's name. You can then choose to deposit money, check the balance, or exit the program.

## Example

```shell
Welcome to the Deposit Calculator!
Enter the account owner's name: John Doe

Options:
1. Deposit Money
2. Check Balance
3. Exit
Choose an option: 1
Enter amount to deposit: 100
Deposited $100.00. New balance is $100.00.

Options:
1. Deposit Money
2. Check Balance
3. Exit
Choose an option: 2
Current balance: $100.00
